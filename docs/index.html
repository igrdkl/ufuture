    About us

    Our volunteer organization is based in Khmelnytsky in the west-central part of Ukraine. We help support regional shelters, hospitals and supply other cities affected by the war with Russia

    The city of Khmelnytsky has become one of the most popular places of refuge for those fleeing the war from the eastern and northern regions of Ukraine.

    We help maintain regional shelters, hospitals and supply other cities affected by the war with Russia
